ABOUT US

VTVSA stands for the Vietnamese Student Association at Virginia Tech. It is a social and cultural organization whose goals are to educate others about Vietnamese culture in addition to creating strong bonds between members and other organizations.

Learn More

CULTURE SHOW

The Vietnamese Student Association's Culture Show is one of the biggest events that happen every year by VSA. With acts such as beautiful traditional dances, jaw dropping modern dances, a stunning fashion show, an engaging script, and MUCH MORE, the show brings together a night full of Vietnamese culture, comedy, and friends. Come and sign up early to perform in the acts, or simply just watch your friends!
MARK YOUR CALENDARS FOR THIS SEASON'S CULTURE SHOW: April 18th, 2015

EVENTS

Throughout the year, VSA engages in fun events 2 to 3 times a month to promote Vietnamese Culture and to bond members together. Some of these events include Pho Night (Free Unlimited Pho for members), Field Day (compete against other classes and houses), Open Mic Night (Open to members to perform), Big/Little Program (get a Big brother, Big sister to show you the ropes), and MUCH MORE!
