Unlimited Phở Night

Free Phở

Pho is a Vietnamese noodle soup consisting of broth, rice noodles called bánh phở, a few herbs, and meat, primarily made with either beef or chicken. FREE for members; $5 for non-members.

Thai Tea

Thai Tea leaves are a combination of east Asian black tea, star anise, cardamom and vanilla bean. It's a combination of strongly brewed tea, dairy and sugar.

Raffles

Buy a raffle ticket for $1. Winners can cut the line for 1 hour to get unlimited bowls of Phở!

Culture Show

Culture

Each culture show depicts a story that is centered around a common theme and educates the audience on the Vietnamese culture.

Art

Along with a captivating storyline, the culture show brings various Vietnamese dances and art forms.

Humor

From the MC's to the on-stage acting, VSA always has a humourous show that will keep you laughing until you leave

Football Tournaments

Turkeybowl

Hosted by George Mason University, Turkey Bowl is a fall football tournament between Vietnamese Student Associations in the Mid-Atlantic Region. The winner of the football tournament wins a trophy and a cash prize towards their philanthropy.

Duke Bowl

Hosted by James Madison University, Duke Bowl is a football tournament held in the spring season. The same schools who participiate in Turkey Bowl also participate in Duke Bowl. The winner of the football tournament wins a trophy and a cash prize towards their philanthropy.

Intramural Sports

The intramural flag football teams consists of a men's, women's, and co-ed.

About

We are the Vietnamese Student Association at Virginia Tech (VTVSA). VTVSA is a student organization looking to promote Vietnamese American culture and to serve as a network for current students and alumni to spark relationships.

Umbrella Organizations

  • Asian American Student Union
  • Mid-Atlantic Union of VSA
  • Union of North American VSA
